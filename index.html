Miao Miao

GET ON Miao Miao, GET ON THE WORLD


A revolutionary social web app

Unlike other social apps where friends are recommended to you based on your friends list, Miao Miao contains a personality test and will recommend people based on the test result.


A fun interacting experience

What's more, Miao Miao also provides you the opportunity to post anonymous questions/posts to your friends. Be bold and ask them things you would not dare to ask in person! Also, you can compete with users from all over the world in a quiz game. Try your best and become the smartest on the server!


Unique chat functions

Miao Miao allow users to connect anyone on the web without worrying about leaking of private information. We do not store the chat history at all! Once the webpage is closed, the chat history is gone forever. No need to be afraid of connecting with others!


Bad language filtering system

Be aware, all these features combined does not mean this web app is a place where you spread hatred language. All posts, questions, and usernames will be filtered.
